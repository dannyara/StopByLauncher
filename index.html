A social app for the modern world

StopBy is an android app that lets you find ane meet people in your area using geolocation services. You are able to create your own unique profile and create posts at location hotspots around the University at Buffalo Area.

Create your own profile

Register with StopBy allows you to not only access the thousands of users currently out meeting each other and making connections, but to create a secure profile that allows you to create posts and interact with those interested in meeting you

Create and view posts

Message other users, schedule to meet up in real life!

Create your own post or search to see who is around you and would like to meet up

Download Now!

    1. Click the download link below

    2. On your android device, find the file and press "install"

    Note, you may have to enable Apps From Unknown Sources in Setting > Security. >"Unknown sources".

    3. Follow the prompts and StopBy Should now be installed
